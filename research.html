Research

I am actively involved in research projects focusing on machine learning and its applications. One of my recent research papers is titled "Developing an artificial intelligence diagnostic tool for paediatric distal radius fractures, a proof of concept study," published in the International Journal of Medical Imaging. You can find the paper here. In this study, we proposed a novel machine learning framework for automated medical image analysis, which demonstrated promising results in various diagnostic tasks. Our research contributes to the advancement of medical imaging technology and the development of intelligent healthcare systems.
